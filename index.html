BY WAY OF INTRODUCTION

AhemNason is Mike Nason, a Scholarly Communications Librarian at the University of New Brunswick in sunny Fredericton, New Brunswick. He cares about things like open access to scholarly research and digital scholarship literacy. That's pretty fun. He works on repositories, outreach, digital projects, and sometimes reference. He also works for the Public Knowledge Project (PKP).

He doesn't "wear many hats" because he thinks that expression is super dumb... unless it is in reference to collecting cycling caps.

In his spare time he plays many instruments in many bands, enjoys downward dogs, IPAs, beards, plaid scarves, steel-framed bicycles... the whole deal. You know.

You can reach him if you try.
He has faith in you.
